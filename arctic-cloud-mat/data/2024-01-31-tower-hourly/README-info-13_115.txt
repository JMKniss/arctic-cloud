Tower, Interpolated hourly measurements to 2.5 and 10m at Met City (ASFG)
[Andreas, E., C. Fairall, P. Guest, O. Persson]
https://data.eol.ucar.edu/dataset/13.115

Summary
This dataset contains processed data from the flux group tower at "Met City"
in the SHEBA ice camp. This dataset is derived from the 5-level hourly
measurements by taking the median fluxes and interpolating the temperature,
humidity and wind to 2.5 and 10 m. This dataset does not contain any of the
variances, etc, but does contain bulk estimates of latent heat flux and
sensible heat flux at 2.5 and 10m.
